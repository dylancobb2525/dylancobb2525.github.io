Bucket List

My top destinations to visit

Florence, Italy

I have never been to Europe and I want to start with Florence because it is where I plan to study abroad. Between the sight seeing and good food, this is at the top of my list.

Paris, France

The food, shopping, and landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower are all things that I hope to experience in the next few years.

Hawaii, USA

I have been to a handful of islands, but this island is definitely a must see for me one day. The nature here looks insane.

Bora Bora, French Polynesia

The water looks crystal clear and seems like a dream for me to experience. This is a trip I hope to take in my later 20s.

Morocco Desert

When I study abroad I hope to visit Morocco and ride a camel in the desert, as it is such a unique experience and something I have never been able to do.

Dubai, UAE

This is a trip I plan to take later on in my life, but the luxury and insane architecture makes it so enticing to visit.
